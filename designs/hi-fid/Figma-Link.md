## 🎯 Design System

### Colors
- Primary: #2196F3 (Blue)
- Secondary: #4CAF50 (Green)
- Success: #4CAF50
- Warning: #FF9800
- Error: #F44336
- Gray Scale: #FAFAFA to #212121

### Typography
- Font: System font stack
- Sizes: 12px to 48px
- Weights: Regular (400), Semibold (600), Bold (700)

### Spacing
- Base Unit: 8px
- Scale: 4, 8, 16, 24, 32, 40, 48, 64px
